Our Services

A comprehensive suite of dispute resolution and expert advisory services delivered with independence, expertise, and professionalism.

Adjudication

Adjudication is a statutory, fast-track dispute resolution mechanism widely used in construction and engineering. It provides parties with a binding interim decision within a defined, short timeframe — typically 28 days from referral — enabling projects to continue without prolonged disruption.

Our adjudicators bring extensive technical and contractual knowledge to provide fair, well-reasoned decisions on payment disputes, variations, extension of time, and other contract issues. All proceedings are conducted in accordance with the applicable statutory scheme and rules of natural justice.

Key Benefits

Binding interim determination within 28 days
Preserves cash flow and project continuity
Cost-effective alternative to arbitration for lower-value disputes
Enforceable immediately unless overturned on final resolution
Technically qualified adjudicators with construction expertise

Arbitration

Arbitration is a private, binding dispute resolution process in which parties submit their dispute to one or more independent arbitrators. The award rendered is legally enforceable in the same manner as a court judgment, including internationally under the New York Convention.

Our arbitrators are Fellows of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, experienced in conducting proceedings under ICC, UNCITRAL, LCIA, and domestic institutional rules. We handle complex multi-party disputes with substantial claims across construction, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

Key Benefits

Legally binding, internationally enforceable awards
Full confidentiality of proceedings and award
Choice of arbitrator with technical expertise
Procedural flexibility tailored to dispute complexity
Neutral forum preferred by international contracting parties

Mediation

Mediation is a voluntary, confidential process in which a skilled, neutral mediator facilitates negotiation between disputing parties to achieve a mutually acceptable settlement. Unlike adjudication or arbitration, the mediator does not impose a decision — the outcome is shaped entirely by the parties themselves.

Our mediators are trained and accredited in facilitative and evaluative mediation techniques. We create structured, safe environments where parties can communicate openly, explore interests, and reach commercial agreements that litigation and arbitration often cannot achieve.

Key Benefits

Preserves commercial relationships and reputations
Faster and less expensive than formal proceedings
Parties control the outcome and terms of settlement
Fully confidential — nothing can be used in later proceedings
High success rate when parties engage in good faith

Dispute Boards

Dispute Boards are standing panels of expert and neutral members established at the outset of long-term infrastructure or construction projects. They visit the project regularly, become familiar with the works and contract, and are available to make recommendations or binding decisions on any disputes that arise during execution.

Resolve Advisory provides experienced Dispute Adjudication Boards (DAB) and Dispute Review Boards (DRB) in compliance with FIDIC and other international frameworks, significantly reducing the risk of disputes reaching arbitration or litigation.

Key Benefits

Real-time dispute prevention and early resolution
Board members build deep knowledge of the project
Reduces risk of major disputes reaching arbitration
FIDIC-compliant advisory and adjudicative functions
Supports project continuity and stakeholder confidence

Expert Determination

Expert Determination is a private, binding dispute resolution process in which an independent expert with specialist knowledge decides a technical, financial, or valuation question. Unlike arbitration, the expert acts on their own knowledge and investigation, not purely on submissions from the parties.

This mechanism is particularly well-suited to disputes involving accounting, valuation, engineering, or other specialist technical matters. The expert's determination is binding unless there is manifest error or fraud.

Key Benefits

Expert-led: determiner has domain expertise
Binding outcome — no further proceedings on the same issue
Faster and less expensive than full arbitration
Ideal for valuation, technical, or quantum disputes
Full confidentiality maintained throughout

Expert Services

Beyond formal dispute resolution processes, Resolve Advisory provides a range of technical expert and advisory services to parties, legal counsel, insurers, and financiers. These services support parties at all stages — from dispute avoidance through to enforcement.

Our expert services include independent quantum assessments, delay and disruption analysis, contract review and advisory, expert witness preparation, and claims documentation review.

Key Benefits

Independent quantum and delay analysis
Expert witness reports for arbitration and court proceedings
Contract review and risk identification advisory
Claims preparation, review, and response support
Dispute avoidance advisory on major projects
